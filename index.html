Project 360 Casino Training

An invite-only training platform for casino staff.

About

Project 360 Casino Training is a mobile and web application that delivers structured training modules, video lessons, and interactive exercises to casino employees. Access is granted by administrators on a per-user basis; the app is not available for public sign-up.

Trainees use the app to work through modules at their own pace, track progress against assigned curricula, and demonstrate competency through in-app exercises. Administrators use the app to assign content, monitor progress, and manage user access.

Authentication

Sign-in is handled exclusively through Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In. Users do not create or manage passwords within the app.
